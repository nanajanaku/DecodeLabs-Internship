## 📈 Key Trends Identified

### Revenue Trend
- Monthly revenue ranged from **$27,909 to $68,069**
- Peak month: **June 2024 ($68,069)**
- Lowest month: **May 2024 ($27,909)**
- No consistent upward growth trend observed

### Product Performance
- **Top products:** Chair & Printer (~$195K each)
- **Lowest product:** Phone (~$152K)

### Order Status
- Cancelled: **250 orders (21%)**
- Returned: **247 orders (21%)**
- **41% of orders never reached the customer**

### Customer Behaviour
- Top referral: **Instagram (259 orders)**
- Top payment: **Online (258 orders)**
- **74% of customers used coupon codes**

---

## 🔍 Outlier Detection
Using the IQR method in Excel:
- **Upper boundary:** $3,330
- **Lower boundary:** -$1,342
- **Outliers detected:** 8 orders
- **Conclusion:** All 8 are genuine high value bulk purchases

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights
1. **41% cancellation/return rate** is a critical concern
2. **Revenue is volatile** with no consistent growth
3. **74% coupon usage** suggests discount dependency
4. **Phone** underperforming vs other products
5. **Instagram** is the most valuable marketing channel
